Trusted environment for entrepreneurs to reflect and receive peer feedback

Cheerpeer provides entrepreneurs social support for their business and professional development. It provides a trusted space for entrepreneurs to reflect on their goals and hold peers accountable through peer feedback.

Designed collaboratively with

What is Cheerpeer?

  • Trust
  • Reflection
  • Accountability

Trust: Cheerpeer is a peer support app specifically designed for small gruops of entrepreneurs to ask questions to each other, share ideas, and get early feedback. It is a place to keep ideas safe and Cheerpeer ensures an encouraging environment as well.

Reflection: Cheerpeer provides unique journal prompts which enable the entrepreneur to reflect on their entrepreneurial journey, and Cheerpeer shows entrepreneur trends overtime.

Accountability: Cheerpeer enables asynchronous peer support which is ongoing. This is helpful for place-based entrpreneur networks, such as incubators or makerspaces to continue support when not physically at the markerspace.
